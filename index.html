The Climbing Cook

My name is Dan Thibodeau. The three things that I enjoy doing most are climbing, cooking, and coding. I started this website as a coding project and it has become a good hobby for me. The website has been created using HTML, CSS, and vanilla Javascript. The idea of the site is to give easily accessible recipes as well as a place for others to share their cooking and find inspiration to cook for the people they love.

Enjoy your stay!
